## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-05-12 | Tampering | `/shop/[auctionId]/page.tsx` | mitigate | `auctionId` from URL is passed directly to `eq(auctionsTable.id, auctionId)` in a Drizzle parameterized query.
